Appalachian Roots • Modern Acoustic

I grew up in Garrett County, Maryland—country/Appalachia—with those roots shaping the way I write, arrange, and perform. Music runs in the family: my dad, Lynn Beckman, has spent his life writing, recording, and performing. I’ve carried that forward on stage and in the studio, blending classic Americana textures with a clean, modern sound.
