The Obligatory About Me Section

I grew up in a house where guitars were basically furniture and someone was always singing — usually whether you wanted them to or not.

I started playing in bar bands at 16 with my oldest brother and have basically been making noise ever since.

These days I split my time between Tim & Katie, Boathouse, and solo shows — mixing country roots, rock energy, and sarcasm.

Those early bar gigs taught me how to perform, how to read a room, and how to survive when a guy named Big Ron demands Free Bird for the fourth time.

I’ve played pretty much everything with strings and my influences are all over the map — Garth Brooks to SRV to Prince to Foo Fighters.

I like songs with heart, guitars with bite, and crowds that don’t take themselves too seriously… because I sure don’t.
